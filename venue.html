Venue

Synchron 2020 will be held in Dammarie-lès-Lys, a small town at the edge of the Fontainebleau forest about 45km south east of the centre of Paris, at the Bois du Lys conference centre.

Coming soon: more info about the centre and accommodation.

Paris to Melun

From the Gare de Lyon station take Ligne R to Melun with a Transilien ticket. The journey normally takes 35 minutes.

Melun to the Venue

See full screen

  • Take a taxi to 380, Chemin du Clocher

  • Bus A from Melun - Gare Thiers (direction Chamlys/Grands Cèdres) to Dammarie-Les-Lys - Jean de la Fontaine.

  • Bus N from Melun - Gare Thiers (direction Peguy) to Cité Lallemand. The bus does not run on Sundays.

The ticket for the train from Paris includes bus travel within one hour of arrival. Otherwise, these buses take the same "Ticket t+" as does the Parisien metro.
