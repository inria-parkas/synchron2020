Watching talks

The slack channel is synchron2020.slack.com. Please join using your full name.

Videoconferencing links will be published before the event. Anyone may connect directly from their own computer.

We would also like to encourage people, where possible, to create local “hubs” with projectors, cameras, microphones, speakers, masks, hydroalcoholic gel, aperitifs, rotten tomatoes, etcetera. Please email Timothy.Bourke@inria.fr with your hub details so that they can be added to the hub list.

Timezones

The online event will take place in Central European Time (UTC+01:00). As is obvious from the table below, this is not very convenient for participants elsewhere in the world. We will do our best to schedule international talks, possibly even starting earlier or later.

Proposing a talk

Talk proposals are now closed.

Anyone may propose a talk within the workshop themes. Please email Timothy.Bourke@inria.fr with Synchron 2020 in the subject line and the title in the message body. Feel free to include a URL for the speaker. The deadline is November 8 (anywhere on earth).

Once we have received all propositions, we will decide how much time to allocate for talks and questions, and we will publish the program.

Talks will be live and not recorded. We may need to adapt the schedule to work around technical problems.
